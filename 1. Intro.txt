The analysis of a comprehensive Airbnb database, emphasizes textual descriptions and 
sentiment analysis, unveils key insights for optimizing listings and potentially expanding 
to new markets. While apartments utilize words that resonate with city tourists looking 
for convenience and nightlife, condominiums emphasize beach vacations through 
evocative and positive language. Houses, on the other hand, target nature lovers yearning 
for privacy with relevant keywords. Through a data-driven understanding of guests' 
preferences by property type, listings descriptions can be tailored to increase bookings.
